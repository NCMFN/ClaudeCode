--- Notes on Limitations vs Paper ---
The paper describes 23 total features. SolRPDS provides raw transaction and liquidity logs. Smart contract authority flags (e.g., mint authority, freeze authority) and social media metadata could not be derived exclusively from SolRPDS. Extracting these would require direct Solana RPC API integration or secondary datasets. The engineered features here represent the maximum expressible proxy features from the given data.
